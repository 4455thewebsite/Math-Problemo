Learn Multi-step Math Equations with Numbers

Welcome to my website where you can practice multi-step math equations with numbers. You will have to solve the equations by following the correct order of operations, which is:

  1. First do any calculations inside parentheses.
  2. Next do all multiplications and divisions, working from left to right.
  3. Finally, do all additions and subtractions, working from left to right.
